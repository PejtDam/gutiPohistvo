who we are

Since 2005, we have been driven by a passion for creativity, perfection, and modern design. We specialize in producing premium furniture for cosmetic stores and commercial offices under the Guti Pohistvo brand, combining craftsmanship and innovation to meet the needs of our clients. We also create custom furniture for your home, helping to create a warm atmosphere where you can cherish moments with family and friends.


how we work

We take pride in using only high-quality materials and advanced technology in our production process. Our flexible solutions allow us to tackle a variety of challenges. By transforming your ideas into reality, we ensure that our customers are completely satisfied with the final product. Whether you need custom designs or inspiration from our previous work, we're here to help you every step of the way.
